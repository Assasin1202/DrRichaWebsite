Introduction

Welcome to my academic homepage. I am a Professor at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at IIT Jodhpur, with adjunct positions at IIIT-Delhi and West Virginia University, USA.

My research focuses on creating trustworthy AI and machine learning systems, particularly in pattern recognition, biometrics, and deep learning. I am passionate about developing robust and explainable AI solutions that can be trusted in real-world applications.

Research

Research Lab

The Image Analysis and Biometrics (IAB) Lab at IIT Jodhpur focuses on research in pattern recognition, machine learning, and computer vision with applications in biometrics and related domains.

Our lab has developed numerous datasets and resources for the research community, including facial recognition systems, healthcare applications, and trustworthy AI solutions.

Research Focus Areas

Deepfake Detection

Our research in deepfake detection focuses on developing robust AI solutions to identify manipulated media across audio, video, and text modalities. We create comprehensive detection frameworks and novel datasets to enhance security in today's digital environment.

Face Recognition

We develop advanced face recognition systems that address challenges of pose variation, illumination changes, and diverse demographic factors. Our algorithms emphasize fairness, bias mitigation, and performance across varied conditions.

Fingerprint Recognition

Our fingerprint recognition research focuses on enhancing accuracy for both high and low-quality samples. We explore contactless fingerprint acquisition and matching algorithms that maintain reliability across various imaging conditions.
